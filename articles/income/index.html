This article refers to the GlideClient project.

GlideClient doesn't make money.

GlideClient is developed for the community as an opensource project

We develop GlideClient for the public, as we don't like that the mincraft 1.8.9 client scene is becoming a monopoly.

We belive in opensource and freedom.


You may ask how we provide GlideClient without taking donations directly for it, and the awnser for that is we have actually spent money on GlideClient that we dont intend to get back.

Our running costs are nearly 0 though, other than the domain.

If you want to support the project, instead we would love for you to contribute code and time to help us develop.

Note: GlideClient is not run by SoarClient and they are run as seperate projects so Soar Premium donations don't/did'nt go to GlideClient

Some of our contributors have ways to dontate to them.

Dont feel presured to donate to them however im sure they would apreciate it.
