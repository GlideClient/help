GlideClient

This article refers to GlideClient 7.2 (7200) and above.

We support the following platforms

x86 (32 Bit)

x64 (64 Bit)

arm64 (64 Bit)

Note: Arm64 support is experimental and you may encounter issues
Linux

x64 (64 Bit)

arm64 (64 Bit)

Note: Arm64 support is experimental and you may encounter issues
Linux

x64 (64 Bit Intel CPUs)

arm64 (64 Bit Apple Silicone CPUs)

Note: Apple Silicone is known to have some issues with rendering howver it is stable.
