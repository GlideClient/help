GlideClient

This article refers to the GlideClient project and website.

Note: This is a mirror of the GlideClient Policy Statement

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. We operate without central servers or databases; we cannot see who you are or how you use the software.

2. Third-Party Handshakes

To function, the software and website initiate "handshakes" with external services. During these connections, your IP address is visible to:

GitHub: For website hosting (GitHub Pages), update checking, and file downloads. (Policy)
Discord: To fetch server statistics for the menu widget and for community interaction if you join our server. (Policy
Microsoft/Mojang: For mandatory account authentication. (Policy)

3. Local Storage & Support

All configurations in the client are stored locally on your machine. We cannot access these unless you voluntarily send them to us (e.g., for troubleshooting on Discord or GitHub).

We do not retain these files after your issue is resolved. You are responsible for deleting any files or messages you have shared on these platforms once they are no longer needed.
